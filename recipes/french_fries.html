Air Fried French Fries

Description

This air fryer French fries recipe includes a cold water-hot water soaking trick that produces super crispy French fries. Made with just four ingredients, these fries rival traditional fries but use a fraction of the oil. Serve with burgers, steak, or chicken with something to dip them in, like honey mustard, spicy ketchup, or garlic aioli.

Igredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the Potatoes: Use a sharp knife to cut each peeled potato into slices, then sticks.
  2. Soak and Drain the Potatoes: Soak the potato sticks in a bowl of cold water for about 5 minutes. Drain, then pour boiling water over the potatoes. Let sit for about 10 minutes. Drain and dry the potatoes.
  3. Season the French Fries: Allow the potato sticks to cool, then transfer to a mixing bowl. Toss them with oil and season with cayenne pepper.
  4. Cook In Air Fryer: Air fry the potato sticks in a double layer in the fryer basket. Cook for 15 minutes, toss, then cook for 10 more minutes. Season with salt.
